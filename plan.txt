Bedroom renovation plan
1. Set a vision
- Measure the space
- Look for inspirations
- Determine the final look of the bedroom and the aesthetic you want to achieve 
- Plan your budget and buy the things and furniture you need
2. Clean up the space
- Take out all furniture and belongings from the room
- Protect the floor before painting
3. Painting
- Repair any holes in the walls and ceiling, prepare the walls for painting
- Paint the walls and ceiling twice, make sure the paint is dry
- Clean up after refurbishment, wash the windows and floor
4. Furnishing & Decor
- Install a new ceiling lamp
- Arrange the furniture
- Add new decorations like curtains, rug, pillows
- Hang pictures
- Tidy up the room
- Make sure everything is in place and enjoy the new room
